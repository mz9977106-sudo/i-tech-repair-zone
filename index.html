Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service framework.

How long does a standard device repair take? ▼
Most common mobile phone and tablet repairs (screen, battery, charging port) are completed on the same day within 1–2 hours. Micro-soldering and liquid damage recovery usually require 24 to 48 hours for deep stress testing.
What kind of warranty comes with the repair? ▼
All our hardware component replacements come standard with a comprehensive coverage warranty that spans up to 12 months, safeguarding against manufacturing faults or performance dips.
Do I need to back up or clear my data prior to submission? ▼
While we strictly preserve your data integrity, we always recommend making a cloud or local backup whenever possible as a standard security precaution.
Are replacement parts original manufacturer components? ▼
We source exclusively OEM-grade or genuine original refurbished components to ensure full functional compatibility, true color response, and original battery efficiency metrics.
What does your 'No Fix, No Fee' policy cover? ▼
If our technical team performs exhaustive diagnostics and determines the device cannot be brought back to functional viability, you will not incur labor charges.
Can I post my device in if I live far away? ▼
Yes! We operate a secure nationwide postal repair service throughout the UK. Reach out via WhatsApp or submit a booking form to receive tracked postal details.
